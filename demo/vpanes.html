Simple transition process

With Remi we begin our transition planning by analyzing your clients’ unique investment profile and evaluating their existing holdings on an individual tax-lot basis. We do this to provide your clients with the benefits of a portfolio based on their tolerance for interest rate, credit and duration risk in their fixed income portfolios.

We deliver a transition analysis with a range of options that examine the tax and tracking-error considerations of moving a legacy portfolio to a customized solution in a risk and tax-efficient manner.

Completion portfolio
The least tax impact with no sells from the legacy portfolio and buys funded only with existing cash.

Optimized portfolio
The best trade-off among risk, returns, and tax liability.

Model portfolio
No considerations of tax liability and best positioned to deliver the model objective.

Customized SMA solutions

Every investor sets investment objectives which are unique to them. One-size-fits-all products are not able to provide the flexibility investors need when it comes to balancing their risk preferences and tax liabilities. The challenge is that customized solutions can be time-consuming to build, operationally complex to manage, and overwhelming to implement across all client portfolios.

Remi enables you to provide your clients with real customization. As an example, a client in our Municipal Income Ladder SMA could customize across these factors:

  • State concentration - National or state-specific emphasis
  • Credit quality - From AAA to A
  • Duration maturity - Three flavors: short, intermediate and long
  • Coupon income - Reinvest or receive the cash
  • Tax loss harvesting - Actively harvest losses year-round

Full-life-cycle tax management

When people talk about growing their clients’ assets, the conversation usually focuses on returns and alpha generation. But for many high-net worth investors, decreasing taxes creates an opportunity to increase net gains. Research has shown that over a 25-year period of active funds’ performance, investors lose nearly 2.5% of their investment returns to taxes annually*. Tax efficient management is a full life cycle process that our customized SMA platform can provide:

  •  Systematic loss-harvesting
    Opportunities can be seized proactively, not just at the end of the year.

  •  Defer gains by holding an asset with embedded gains
    If sold, the asset with an embedded gain incurs tax, sometimes waiting is beneficial.

  •  Tax-lot level decision-making
    Holistic tax optimization provided by our robust tax management technology.

  •  Managing highly appreciated assets
    In the later years of an account, the emphasis will shift to managing a combination of highly appreciated assets and new assets in the portfolio in tax-aware fashion.
  •  Tax liability management
    Managing the overall tax liability of of the client by paying attention to client taxes on other accounts and prior year loss carry-forward.

  •  Intelligent management of asset holding periods
    Avoid generating short-term gains by factoring in holding periods.

  •  Smarter cash withdrawals
    Drawing down from the account periodically or one-off in a tax-sensitive fashion.

  •  Avoiding violation of the wash-sale rule
    Replacing assets sold to offset capital gains with a similar sectoral or risk profile security.
