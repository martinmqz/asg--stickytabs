Simple transition process

With Remi we begin our transition planning by analyzing your clients’ unique investment profile and evaluating their existing holdings on an individual tax-lot basis. We do this to provide your clients with the benefits of a portfolio based on their tolerance for interest rate, credit and duration risk in their fixed income portfolios.

Customized SMA solutions

Every investor sets investment objectives which are unique to them. One-size-fits-all products are not able to provide the flexibility investors need when it comes to balancing their risk preferences and tax liabilities. The challenge is that customized solutions can be time-consuming to build, operationally complex to manage, and overwhelming to implement across all client portfolios.

Full-life-cycle tax management
